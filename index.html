Welcome to Animals In Need

Animals in Need began with the satos (stray dogs) of Puerto Rico. Our first rescued dog Macho, named after the beach he was rescued from, started it all. He was found on the beach in Puerto Rico, his face covered in dry blood, he'd lost an eye, part of an ear, tail broken in two places, he was near death. He was taken to a local vet to recieve treatment for his injuries and mange, which is very common in Puerto Rico. It took several months to nurse him back to health, after he was flown to Washington where we gave him the chance for the life he deserved.

Seeing the change and receiving the love and affection of a sato, made us realize that there are hundreds of dogs like Macho in Puerto Rico. Dogs that are smart, loving, and just looking for a home to share their love. We continue to help other satos like Macho that are amazing survivors, and the lives they live out after rescue is always awe-inspring.
